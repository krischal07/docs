RestroX is an outlet-owned POS provider in Samparka. Each outlet gets exactly one RestroX integration, one Integration Key, one webhook token, and one restaurant binding.

<Info>
RestroX launches directly on the outlet-owned architecture. There is no RestroX migration path, no dual-mode setup, and no RestroX-specific compatibility layer.
</Info>

## Outlet-Owned Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Integration">
    Merchant creates the RestroX integration with `provider`, `storeId`, and `outletId`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Integration Key">
    Samparka generates an Integration Key and webhook token immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Restaurant">
    RestroX calls the partner connect route and binds one restaurant to the outlet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bind Restaurant Identity">
    Samparka stores the binding on `PosIntegration.external_location_id` and `external_location_name`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send First Sale">
    A valid sale to `POST /webhook/restrox/{token}` activates the integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Refunds and future sales continue on the same outlet-owned binding.
  </Step>
</Steps>
