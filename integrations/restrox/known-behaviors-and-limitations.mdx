---
title: Known Behaviors And Limitations
description: Production behaviors RestroX engineers should understand before going live.
sidebarTitle: Known Behaviors
---

- RestroX is always outlet-owned in Samparka.
- `sync-locations` is implemented as a skipped no-op for RestroX.
- reconnect preserves the existing Integration Key and webhook token.
- rebind is blocked until the integration is disconnected.
- the first valid `sale.completed` event promotes `CONNECTED` to `ACTIVE`.
- a restaurant mismatch webhook returns `409` and sets health to `ERROR`.
