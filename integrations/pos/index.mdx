## Onboarding Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Integration Key">
    Confirm the Samparka Integration Key for the outlet-owned POS integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Location">
    Call the connect endpoint with `integrationKey`, `externalLocationId`, and optional `externalLocationName`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhook">
    Point POS at the Samparka webhook endpoint after the integration status is `CONNECTED`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Test Sale">
    Use the documented phone-first sale fixture to verify that an accepted request returns a `200` acknowledgment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Response">
    Confirm the expected success message, verify customer lookup by phone, and resend the same payload once to validate idempotency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Refund">
    Send a `refund.created` payload that references the original sale identifier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Finish the integration checklist, then promote the connection to production traffic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
