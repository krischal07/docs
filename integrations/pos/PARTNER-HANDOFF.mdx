## Recommended Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read Overview">
    Review the onboarding map and the canonical guide before configuring anything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Restaurant">
    Use the connect endpoint first so one POS restaurant is bound to one outlet-owned Samparka integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhook">
    Use the webhook endpoint and token after the integration status is `CONNECTED`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Test Sale">
    Follow the quick start and testing guide to send the initial `order.completed` payload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify ACTIVE">
    Confirm the first valid sale moves the integration to `ACTIVE`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Customer">
    Search for the customer created or resolved by the sale and confirm it belongs to the expected store or business.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Loyalty Outcome">
    Fetch the customer details, confirm loyalty fields are populated, and verify points were awarded for the sale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Duplicate Handling">
    Repost the same payload once and confirm the duplicate-safe response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Refund">
    Send the refund payload that reuses the original sale identifier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Checklist">
    Run the production-readiness checks before switching to live traffic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Use the checklist and verification materials as your signoff package.
  </Step>
</Steps>
