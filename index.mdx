## What You Can Build

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect a POS or Platform">
    Use the connect endpoint to link a restaurant or outlet to Samparka Loyalty with an integration key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stream Transaction Events">
    Send sale, refund, and void events over a secure webhook so Samparka can award and track loyalty points in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify Customers">
    Resolve customers by phone number — no account required at the point of sale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle Edge Cases">
    Use idempotency keys, blocked-location rules, and duplicate detection to keep your integration reliable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Complete the integration checklist, verify responses, and promote to production traffic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
